Gastrointestinal Cancers Kill ~40 Australians Per Day

Gastrointestinal cancer refers to a family of cancers which affect the digestive system. Gastrointestinal cancer is the most common form of cancer and it impacts both men and women. Annually, approximiatle 30,000 Australians are diganosed with some form of gastrointestinal cancer. Specific types of gastrointestinal cancers include:



  • Liver cancer

  • Stomach cancer

  • Gallbladder cancer

  • Pancreatic cancer

  • Colorectal cancer

  • Bowel cancer

  • Anal cancer





      Source: AGITG, 2021
