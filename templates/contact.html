Our Vision and Mission

In an effort to raise awareness surrounding gastrointestinal cancer and galvanize potential cancer victims to take early action, we created a predictive model that aims to provide free and accessible gastrointestinal cancer diagnostics for early detections and second opinions.


This website was created to bridge users with our predictive model while providing digestible information about gastrointestinal cancer and directing users to trusted sources for further information and help.





Our Predictive Model

Our Convolutional Neural Network model is trained on 192,312 unique image patches taken from histopathological images of
colorectal cancer and gastric cancer patients.





Source: Kaggle, 2021

Combined with an application of fine-tuning techniques from our end, our model is optimised to make a prediction of either MSIMUT (cancerous tumour) or MSS (non-cancerous tumour) when given histopathological images of the gastrointestinal region.

Our model was tested on four performance metrics and achieved:

Accuracy: 75%

Sensitivity: 82%

Specificity: 76%

AUC: 0.74
